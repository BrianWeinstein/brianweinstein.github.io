I’m Brian.

I manage a team of Data Scientists and Product Analysts at Slice. Our team works on our consumer apps, restaurant software, and internal tools; guiding the product direction and helping teams make informed product decisions using data.

I’m passionate about the intersection of data science, product, and research: from understanding and quantifying user problems to developing and testing product solutions. I also love people management: mentoring my team in data science and communication best practices, finding new opportunities so they can grow, and building a thoughtful and inclusive team culture.

Previously I managed the Data Science & Research team at WeWork, was a Data Scientist at WeWork, and was a Data Scientist at 1stdibs. I have my Master’s degree in applied math and my Bachelor’s in physics.

Outside of data, I enjoy making pottery, eating froyo, baking cookies, and running.
